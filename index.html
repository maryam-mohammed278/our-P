About FCI Fayoum University



Republican Decree No. 267 of 2006 was issued, which includes the establishment of the Faculty of Computers and Information at Fayoum University. The Supreme Council of Universities agreed, in its session on 9/3/2006, to start studying at the college, starting from the 2006-2007 academic year.


The logo of the College of Computers and Information consists of two parts. The first part represents the Fayoum driveways, and the second part represents information technology. It consists of two numbers (0, 1) and they represent the transition in information technology from the analog system to the digital.

College vision: The Faculty of Computers and Information, Fayoum University, aspires to advance and excel in the field of education and scientific research, to achieve a prominent position locally and internationally, and to develop the information society while adhering to professional ethics.
College message: The Faculty of Computers and Information, Fayoum University, seeks to prepare specialized and distinguished cadres in the field of computers and information technology,
qualified and equipped with theoretical and applied foundations in the field of specialization and capable of continuous learning to compete in the labor market within a framework of ethical values,
and to contribute to the development of scientific research and local and international cooperation to serve the community.

Fayoum University

In 1975, the Faculty of Education was established in Fayoum as one of the colleges affiliated with Cairo University. In 1976, the Faculty of Agriculture was established in Fayoum. In 1981, the Faculty of Engineering was established. In 1983, a republican decree was issued to consider the Fayoum university branch an independent branch of Cairo University. Finally, the dream of the people of Fayoum was fulfilled with the issuance of Republican Decree No(84) for the year 2005 to establish Fayoum University, and then the Republican Decree No(193) for the year 2005 to establish the Faculties of Medicine and Archeology, and the two decisions culminated in the decision to appoint Prof. Dr. Galal Mustafa Saeed as President of Fayoum University. click here... to go to Fayoum University website.
